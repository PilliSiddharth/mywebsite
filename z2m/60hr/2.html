Market Segment and Needs during the Pandemic

In this Assignment, I am going to look into the Market Needs and the Preventive Measures that should be taken during the Pandemic & after the Lockdown on April 14th in India.

Devices/Equipment/Processes for the Prevention of SPREADING OF COVID - 19.

Mass Development of Testing Kits for COVID - 19

Laboratory testing for the respiratory COVID-19 Disease includes methods that detect the presence of virus and those that detect antibodies produced in response to infection.

Detection of antibodies can be used both for clinical purposes and population surveillance.

Development
Many Countries including India, USA and South Korea have developed their own testing kits.

South Korea was the first country to develop a kit and USA for now has the fastest detection Kit.

With high populated countries like India, there is a need for Mass Development of these kits for the satisfaction of their People.

Availability of Ventilators for Affected Patients

A ventilator is a machine that provides mechanical ventilation by moving breathable air into and out of the lungs, to deliver breaths to a patient who is physically unable to breathe, or breathing insufficiently.

Modern ventilators are computerized microprocessor controlled machines, but patients can also be ventilated with a simple, hand-operated bag valve mask.

These devices are of extreme use to COVID - 19 Patients suffering from Diabetes and other related diseases.

Development
Many Celebrities are working for the donation of such devices. Elon Musk and his companies started producing such Ventilators at his factories.

Research is also going on for the proper development of ventilators at an affordable level quickly.

Respirators and Face Shields

Respirators (not masks) are of extreme need during this situation. There is a worldwide demand for the production of these ventilators for people working on the frontline in Health Care.

Development
Many Private Companies are working towards developing an affordable mask for protection against the Pandemic.
Mass Production is also going on.

Awareness

In India, there is a huge need for the development of Medical Systems and Technology among the masses, this can cure the huge amount of unawareness among people regarding the Pandemic.

Many Educational Institutions, members of the UN (such as WHO) as well as the Media are hoping to do this by organising various events like Janata Curfew, which was organised by the Government for the appreciation of the Health Care Specialists working frontline during the pandemic.

Development of Vaccine

The ultimate villain of the COVID - 19 is the development of a vaccine. Vaccines consists of various cells of the antibodies in a particular sequence.

They also consist of an algorithm which plays a vital role in the vaccine.

Development
Many countries in the world have started the development of the vaccine. Many reports say that countries such as Australia and USA have developed an algorithm for the vaccine.

Even if a vaccine is developed, it takes almost an year to release it to public.
