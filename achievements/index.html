Achievements

These are few of my achievements :D

Hosted Xplorer'24 Hackathon, after the success of Xplorer'23

I'm the founder of Hack Club at Gatik Junior College, and I have hosted Telangana's first High School Student Hackathon which had an audience of 150+ students coming across Telangana and creating projects on solving SDGs (Sustainable Development Goals) using tech.

Hastavaani, A.I powered Sign Language Translator

I have worked in developing Hastavaani, an A.I assistive technology gloves which is in great use to assist the deaf and dumb population of India by converting Sign Language gestures which are captured through the gloves to traditional languages like English, Hindi, Marathi, Telugu as speech. Hastavaani has also received funding of a grant of 80,000 rupees from Gatik Junior College to help in the continuity of work in the project.

Runner-up in Knowvation's 24 Hour Hackathon in THub, Hyderabad

We've came up as a runner up in Knowvation's 24 Hour Hackathon for AutoFocus, an A.I browser which avoids distractions.

HPE Code Wars 2024

I have secured a rank of 41 out of 19,000 participants in HPE Code Wars 2024, a coding problem solving competition hosted by HPE (Hewlett Packard Enterprise).

Samsung Solve for Tomorrow - Top 10

I've came in Samsung's Solve for Tomorrow - Top 10 in 2024. I've created sahAIam, an A.I personal assistant for the elderly which works on their feature phone. Samsung has taken me on a trip to Bangalore, Delhi, & Noida showing around their research centers and were offering design training programs to better my project for the final pitch.

Byjus Young Genius Award

I received the Byjus Young Genius Award for the year 2022. I got invited to the Byjus Young Genius program hosted in collaboration with News18 TV to show off my data science skills.
